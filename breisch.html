Kathryn Breisch, Founder


Kathryn founded Uncover Education LLC to help schools develop social issue-oriented curricula and culturally responsive teachers, especially in upper-level STEM content areas. Having worked in public, private, graduate, and virtual schools, and at the district-level, Kathryn's vast breadth of experiences inform her holistic and innovative approach to creating individualized consulting plans for each client. Kathryn earned a Masters in Education from Johns Hopkins University and completed an additional 18 credits, earning a license in Education Administration.
